About Us

Kro.co Specialty Coffee was founded by Mr. Marc Pavillar in November 2023 with a simple vision, Camarines Norte. to bring together great coffee, delicious food, and a welcoming space for the local community of Daet, Camarines Norte.

Located at The Legacy Building, F. Pimentel Avenue, Kro.co Specialty Coffee offers an warm and welcoming space where every customer can relax, connect, and enjoy handcrafted beverages made with passion. We take pride in our specialty coffee and signature drinks such as the Bicol Express Latte, a creative blend inspired by local flavors, the Yuzu Passion Matcha, and the refreshing Kiwi Lime Soda.

Beyond our beverages, we also serve a variety of meals and snacks, including favorites like Carbonara, and Crispy Chicken Sandwich. Perfect for pairing with your favorite drink.

At Kro.co, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage, it’s an experience that connects people. Whether you’re here to study, meet friends, or simply unwind, our cafe is your cozy corner in Daet where comfort meets creativity.

The name “Kro.co” holds a special story, it was inspired by Coco, our beloved dog who used to love chewing on anything in sight, earning the nickname “crocodile.” From that playful memory came the name Kro.co, a blend of fun, warmth, and personality, just like the coffee experience we aim to share with everyone.

We continue to grow with our community by promoting our offerings through Facebook and Instagram, ensuring that every coffee lover can discover what makes Kro.co Specialty Coffee a unique and memorable destination.
